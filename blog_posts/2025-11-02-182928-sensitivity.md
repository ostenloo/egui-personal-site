I often wonder about why I am the way I am. 

Our very existence? Very puzzling. 

And my emotions? Very overwhelming. 

I am a sensitive person. This does not mean I am feeble or easily defeated. No, in the face of adversity, I will outlast almost everyone you know.

I am sensitive because it is the way I am wired. I cannot help but feel, and feel deeply. It is not something I chose, but it is something that I must learn to live with. 

This sensitivity causes me caution. It begets a slow, ever-churning mill of sensation into intention into action. All the steps must align, and come in the correct time.
