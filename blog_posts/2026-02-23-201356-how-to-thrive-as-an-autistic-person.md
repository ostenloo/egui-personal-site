Autism has its challenges, and its often not easy to live a happy or fulfilling life whilst having it. However, I don't think that its impossible to do so. In my opinion, this is the optimal approach to living life as an Autistic Individual. 

# Defining Problem Scope 

As in all forms of problem-solving, so does it apply that living with Autism can be configured as a problem to be solved. So that involves defining a problem scope. 

We can go about this by putting the effects of Autism into 3 categories: 

1. Hard limits. What is *immutable* about the way Autism shows up in your life? 
2. Soft limits. What is *made more difficult* by the way Autism shows up in your life? 
3. Augmentations. What is *raised to a higher degree* by the way Autism shows up in your life? 

I mainly am focusing on effects that can be observed, since I am also aware that ultimately, it all stems from the way the brain works. 

# Traits that affect Quality of Life outside of Autism 

There are some other traits that exist independently of Autism that also have an effect on the Quality of Life as an Autistic person. And they are: 

1. IQ 
2. Physical Attractiveness 
3. Temperament 
4. Gender/Sexuality 
5. Chronic Illnesses 
6. Other Neurodivergence or Mental Disorders

I personally believe that IQ is very important in the ease and efficacy of understanding and applying the approach I describe. 

Other traits are outside the scope of this blog post.
